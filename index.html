About TrendNest

Contact →

TrendNest was founded to bring considered, high quality objects into everyday life. We partner directly with makers and small ateliers to bring limited runs and sustainably produced pieces to our collection. Each item is selected for craftsmanship, material honesty, and longevity.

We believe in slower cycles — fewer, better purchases that last. If you want to learn about a product's origin, reach out via contact, and we'll happily share provenance and care instructions.
